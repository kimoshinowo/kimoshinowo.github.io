About me

I’m a software developer with 5 years’ experience in Web Development and a Master’s degree with Distinction in Computer Science. Having worked with both the front and back, I enjoy doing a bit of both, but spend most of my time doing front end development.

Starting my university journey doing a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Philosophy, I took part in a Code First Girls web development course during my first year and found a passion for web development. Wanting to delve further into the field, I worked as a web developer during my placement year for my undergraduate degree, and enjoyed it so much that I continued on to do a Computer Science degree!

My favourite languages to develop with are Python, HTML, CSS (SCSS), Twig and JavaScript (in which I've dabbled in jQuery and React), but I also have experience with PHP and Java. Other technologies I have experience with include: NPM, Gulp, Symfony, Webpack, MySQL, Git, Docker, Jupyter Notebook and AWS.
